About Weapon Special Rule Costs

When creating custom weapons, you can add these special rules to adjust the weapon's capabilities. Each rule has an associated cost modifier:

  • Positive cost (+) makes the weapon more expensive
  • Negative cost (-) makes the weapon less expensive

The total cost of all special rules should be added to the base weapon cost.
